Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how TodaySays (hereinafter referred to as "the App") collects, uses, and protects personal information.

The App complies with the Personal Information Protection Act, the Act on Promotion of Information and Communications Network Utilization and Information Protection, and Google Play policies.

1. Personal Information Collected

The App does not collect any personally identifiable information that directly identifies users. However, the following information may be collected automatically.

(1) Advertising-related Information

  • Advertising ID (Google Advertising ID)
  • Ad impressions, clicks, and usage records

This information is collected through the Google AdMob advertising SDK and is used for providing advertisements and measuring advertising performance.

(2) Location Information (Optional)

The App may use device location information to provide weather information.

Location information is used temporarily only on the device and is not stored on servers or directly shared with third parties.

※ Even if location permission is not granted, the basic function of the App (clock) can still be used.

2. Purpose of Collecting and Using Personal Information

The App uses personal information only for the following purposes:

  • Providing location-based weather information
  • Providing advertisements and measuring advertising performance
  • Improving service quality and analyzing errors

3. Retention and Use Period of Personal Information

The App does not separately store personal information.

Advertising IDs and related data are processed in accordance with Google AdMob policies.

4. Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties

The App does not provide users’ personal information to external parties in principle. However, exceptions apply in the following cases:

  • Recipient: Google LLC
  • Purpose of Provision: Providing advertisements and measuring advertising performance
  • Related Policy: Google Privacy Policy

5. Outsourcing of Personal Information Processing

For smooth service operation, the App may outsource personal information processing as follows:

Service Provider Scope of Outsourced Work
Google AdMob Ad delivery and ad performance analysis

6. User Rights and Choices

Users may exercise the following rights at any time:

  • Resetting the advertising ID or limiting personalized ads through device settings
  • Granting or denying location permissions

Example path: Android Settings → Privacy → Ads

7. Protection of Children’s Personal Information

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13.

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If such information is identified, it will be deleted immediately.

8. Technical and Administrative Measures for Personal Information Protection

The App takes the following measures to protect personal information:

  • Using only the minimum necessary information
  • Complying with the latest Android security policies
  • Using only trusted external SDKs

9. Changes to the Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be revised due to changes in laws or services.

Any changes will be announced through the App or the store page.
